Title: Flatmates Bill
Description:
App that gets as inputs the amount of a bill for a period, and the days that each flatmate is at home
Returns how much each one has to pay
Generates a PDF report stating their names, period and how much each needs to pay

Objects (we can gess them by looking at the nouns that we have in our description):
- Bill ("amount" and "period" are attributes of the bill)
- Flatmate (name, days in house as attributes, pays(bill) as method)
- PDF Report (filename, generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
